Virtual CFO Support for Manufacturing & Trading Businesses

Manufacturing and trading businesses often face intense working capital pressure, inventory lock-ups, and delayed receivables. VCFO support focuses on cash discipline and financial control.

Common Financial Challenges

  • High inventory blocking cash
  • Receivables stretching beyond agreed terms
  • Vendor payment pressure
  • Mismatch between profit and cash

How a Virtual CFO Helps

  • Working capital cycle analysis
  • Cash flow visibility and forecasting
  • Receivables and payables discipline
  • Decision support for growth vs liquidity
