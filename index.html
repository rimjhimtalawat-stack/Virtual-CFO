About Me

I work as a Virtual CFO with MSMEs and growing companies to bring financial clarity, discipline, and structure into their finance function. My role goes beyond accounting and compliance — focusing on cash flow control, working capital management, risk identification, and decision support.

I partner closely with business owners and finance teams to ensure that financial data is converted into clear insights, priorities, and actions that support sustainable business growth and predictable performance.

Who This Is For / Not For

This is a strong fit if:

  • Your business is growing but cash visibility is weak
  • You have accounting data but lack decision-ready insights
  • Finance feels reactive instead of controlled
  • You want senior financial oversight without full-time hiring

This may not be suitable if:

  • You only require bookkeeping or return filing
  • You are seeking short-term accounting support
  • You are unwilling to implement financial discipline

How I Work as a Virtual CFO

  1. Understand the business model and financial structure
  2. Create visibility across cash, receivables, and payables
  3. Identify risks early and highlight priorities
  4. Support decisions with financial implications
  5. Review monthly to ensure control and predictability

Real Dashboards Used During Engagements

Cash Flow Dashboard
Monthly and rolling visibility of inflows, outflows, and liquidity position.

Working Capital Dashboard
Receivables and payables ageing to identify cash blockages and pressure points.

Risk & Control Dashboard
Early warning indicators across cash stress, compliance, and operational risks.
