About the Virtual CFO Practice

This practice partners with MSMEs and growing companies to bring financial structure, predictability, and discipline into the finance function. The focus is not accounting output, but financial control and decision clarity.

Engagements are designed to support business owners and finance teams with cash flow visibility, working capital control, risk identification, and structured financial decision-making.

Who This Is For / Not For

This is a strong fit if:

  • Your business is growing but cash visibility is unclear
  • You have accounting data but lack decision-ready insights
  • Finance feels reactive instead of controlled
  • You want senior oversight without hiring full-time

This may not be suitable if:

  • You only require bookkeeping or compliance filing
  • You are seeking short-term accounting support
  • You are unwilling to implement financial discipline

What Clients Typically Struggle With

Cash shortages despite reported profits

Delayed collections and uncontrolled receivables

Unclear visibility on payables and obligations

Compliance surprises impacting cash

How the VCFO Framework Works

  1. Assess business model and financial structure
  2. Create cash flow and working capital visibility
  3. Identify risks early across liquidity and compliance
  4. Support management decisions with clear implications
  5. Review monthly to ensure discipline and control
